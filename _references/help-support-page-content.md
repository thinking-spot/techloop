### Before Renting FAQs

**Q: How does the security deposit work?**

A: **We hold (not charge) a refundable deposit on your card.**

**How it works:**
1. When you sign up, we authorize a hold on your card (like hotels do)
2. This appears as "pending" on your statement
3. We never actually charge this amount (unless device is damaged/lost)
4. When you return the device in good condition, the hold is released
5. Your bank refunds the amount within 5-7 business days

**Deposit amounts:**
- **Starter plan:** $200 (1 device)
- **Explorer plan:** $350 (2 devices)  
- **Power User:** $200 per device

**Refund rate:** 98% of renters get 100% back

**What's covered:** Normal wear and tear (scratches, scuffs, worn pads)

**What's not covered:** Cracked screens, broken frames, water damage, loss/theft (unless you have insurance)

**What "brand new" means:**

### Technical FAQs

**Q: My device won't turn on. What should I do?**

A: **Troubleshoot in this order:**

**Step 1: Check battery (most common cause)**
- Plug into charger for 15 minutes
- Look for charging indicator light
- Try different cable/adapter (cable might be bad)
- Try different outlet (outlet might be dead)

**Step 2: Force restart**
- **Meta Ray-Ban:** Hold power button 10 seconds
- **XREAL:** Unplug and replug USB-C cable
- **Oura/Samsung Ring:** Place on charger, wait 1 minute
- **Nothing Ear:** Place in case, close lid, wait 30 seconds

**Step 3: Check for damage**
- Any visible cracks or breaks?
- Was device dropped recently?
- Any water exposure?

**Step 4: Contact us**
- If none of above work, it's likely defective
- Text/email us: [support contact]
- We'll troubleshoot further or send replacement
- Replacement ships same day (arrives in 2-3 days)

**Defective devices:** Covered 100% under warranty. You're not charged anything.

---

**Q: My XREAL glasses show a blurry display. How do I fix it?**

A: **90% of the time, it's a fit issue. Here's the fix:**

**Problem 1: Wrong nose pad size**

**Symptoms:** Entire display blurry, edges especially

**Fix:**
1. Remove current nose pads (gently pull off)
2. Try the next size (Small â†’ Medium â†’ Large)
3. Snap on new pads
4. Put on glasses, check if sharper

**Most people:** Medium works (pre-installed)  
**Smaller faces:** Need Small  
**Larger faces:** Need Large

---

**Problem 2: IPD (Interpupillary Distance) mismatch**

**Symptoms:** Double vision, eyestrain after 30 min

**Explanation:** XREAL is fixed at 64mm IPD. Your eyes might be wider/narrower.

**Check your IPD:**
- Visit optometrist (they measure free)
- Use iPhone app "EyeMeasure" (decent accuracy)
- Average: 60-68mm (most people fall here)

**If your IPD is 67mm+:** XREAL might not work well for you (eyestrain)

**Solution:** Contact us for swap to different device (Meta Ray-Ban has no IPD issues)

---

**Problem 3: Dirty lenses**

**Symptoms:** Spots, smudges, overall haziness

**Fix:**
1. Use included microfiber cloth
2. Gentle circular motions
3. Don't use shirt/paper towels (scratches lens)
4. For stubborn smudges: Lens cleaning spray (eyeglass stores)

---

**Problem 4: Source device resolution wrong**

**Symptoms:** Display looks stretched or pixelated

**Fix:**
1. Check your laptop/phone settings
2. Set display output to 1920x1080 (native XREAL resolution)
3. Avoid scaling (use 100%)

**If still blurry after all this:** Contact us, might be defective unit.

---

**Q: My Oura Ring isn't syncing. What should I do?**

A: **Follow this troubleshooting sequence:**

**Step 1: Check Bluetooth**
- Is Bluetooth enabled on your phone?
- Is phone within 30 feet of ring?
- Try turning Bluetooth off/on

**Step 2: Force sync in app**
- Open Oura app
- Pull down on home screen (refresh gesture)
- Wait 30 seconds for sync

**Step 3: Restart ring**
- Place ring on charger
- Wait 1 minute
- Remove from charger
- Check app again

**Step 4: Re-pair ring**
- Oura app â†’ Settings â†’ Forget Device
- Remove ring from charger
- Wait 10 seconds
- Place back on charger
- Oura app â†’ Add New Device
- Follow pairing process

**Step 5: Restart phone**
- Turn phone completely off
- Wait 30 seconds
- Turn back on
- Check if syncing works

**If still not working:**
- Contact us (might be defective)
- Or contact Oura support directly (they're responsive)

**Most common cause:** Phone Bluetooth got confused. Restart phone fixes 80% of cases.
