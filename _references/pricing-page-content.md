## Copy Principles Used Throughout

1. **Lead with value, not price**
   - "$1.60/day" is more palatable than "$48/month"
   - "Try $400 devices" emphasizes savings

2. **Remove friction at every step**
   - "Cancel anytime" repeated 6 times
   - "No credit check" prominently displayed
   - "Brand new devices" addresses used device concerns

3. **Use comparison anchors**
   - "Compare to buying..." shows savings
   - "vs. Affirm financing..." shows value
   - "Grover charges $45/mo for used devices" positions premium

4. **Address objections preemptively**
   - FAQ addresses: damage, swaps, stock, commitment
   - Social proof shows real users succeeding
   - Trust badges alleviate security concerns

5. **Create urgency (but not fake scarcity)**
   - "New devices added monthly" (FOMO on new tech)
   - "1,000+ users already trying" (social proof)
   - No fake countdown timers or "only 3 spots left"

6. **Emphasize flexibility as core value prop**
   - Swap system explained thoroughly
   - Rent-to-own detailed with math
   - Pause subscription option highlighted

7. **Transparent pricing (no hidden fees)**
   - All costs shown upfront
   - Deposit explanation clear
   - Additional swap fees disclosed
   - No surprises at checkout
