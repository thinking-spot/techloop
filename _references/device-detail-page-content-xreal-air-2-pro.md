**Headline:** Smooth as butter for fast-paced games

**Body:**

**Real-World Gaming Use Cases:**
- **Steam Deck:** Play Elden Ring, Hades, or Cyberpunk on giant screen from anywhere

### Common First-Week Questions

**Q: Why is the screen blurry?**
A: Three possible causes:
1. Dirty lenses (clean with microfiber cloth)
2. Wrong nose pad size (try larger/smaller pads)
3. IPD mismatch (XREAL is fixed at 64mm‚Äîif your eyes are far apart, it may not be perfect)

**Q: Why do I see faint ghosting/double image?**
A: Usually fit issue. Glasses need to sit perfectly centered. Adjust nose pads and temple angle. If persists, might be IPD mismatch.

**Q: Can I use these while walking around?**
A: Technically yes, but dangerous. No head tracking (display stays fixed). Walk while watching = motion sickness + trip hazard. These are for stationary use (sitting, lying down).

**Q: How do I adjust brightness?**
A: Brightness controlled by source device (phone, laptop), not glasses. Turn down your phone brightness if too bright.

**Q: Can I use these in direct sunlight outdoors?**
A: No. Even at 100% tint, sunlight washes out the display. Use indoors or shaded outdoor areas only.
