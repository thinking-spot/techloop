## Section 6: How to Use (Getting Started Guide)

**Section Header:**
## Getting Started: Your First 48 Hours

**Subheadline:**
Most people are capturing POV videos within 30 minutes. Here's exactly how to get started.

---

### Step 1: Unbox & Charge (15 minutes)

**What to Do:**
1. Open box, remove glasses from charging case
2. Plug charging case into USB-C cable
3. Place glasses back in case to charge
4. Wait 15 minutes for initial charge (or use immediately at 40% battery)

**Why:**
Glasses ship at ~40% battery. Quick charge gets you to 70%, enough for setup.

---

### Step 2: Download Meta View App (5 minutes)

**What to Do:**
1. Open App Store (iPhone) or Google Play (Android)
2. Search "Meta View"
3. Download and install (free)
4. Open app, create account (or log in with Facebook/Instagram)

**Why:**
App required for pairing, syncing photos/videos, and software updates.

---

### Step 3: Pair Glasses to Phone (10 minutes)

**What to Do:**
1. Open Meta View app
2. Tap "Pair New Device"
3. Hold power button on glasses for 5 seconds (LED blinks white)
4. Select "Meta Ray-Ban" from Bluetooth list in app
5. Follow on-screen pairing instructions
6. Allow Bluetooth, location, camera permissions

**Why:**
One-time setup. After this, glasses auto-connect when you turn them on.

**Troubleshooting:**
- If glasses don't appear: Make sure they're charged and within 3 feet of phone
- If pairing fails: Turn Bluetooth off/on, restart glasses
- Still stuck? Text us: [support number]

---

### Step 4: Adjust Fit & Controls (10 minutes)

**Physical Fit:**
1. Adjust nose pads for comfort (pinch gently to widen/narrow)
2. Temple tips should rest comfortably behind ears (not too tight)
3. Lenses should sit at natural eye level

**Learn Touch Controls:**
- **Single tap** (right temple): Take photo
- **Tap and hold** (right temple): Record video (up to 60 seconds)
- **Double tap** (right temple): Play/pause music
- **Swipe forward** (right temple): Volume up
- **Swipe backward** (right temple): Volume down
- **Say "Hey Meta":** Activate AI assistant (hands-free)

**Practice:**
Take 5-10 photos of random objects. Record a few test videos. Get comfortable with tap timing.

---

### Step 5: Capture Your First Real Moment (Whenever You're Ready)

**Ideas for First POV Video:**
- Cooking dinner (hands-free recipe following)
- Dog walk (capture their perspective)
- Workout (see what you see during lifting)
- Commute (hands-free vlog)
- Playing with kids (capture without holding phone)

**Pro Tips:**
- Frame shot with slight downward tilt (camera sits above your eyes)
- Lighting matters (indoors can be dim, outdoors is best)
- Narrate what you're doing (built-in mics capture your voice)
- Keep videos <30 seconds (easier to share)

**Q: Can people tell when I'm recording them?**

A: **Technically yes, practically no.**

**What indicates recording:**
- Small white LED on right temple (lights up when camera active)
- LED is visible to people next to you in bright light
- LED is harder to see in direct sunlight or dim environments

**Reality check:**
- Most people don't notice the LED (it's tiny)
- Only tech-savvy people look for it
- In most situations, people assume you're wearing sunglasses

**Ethical use:**
- Tell people if recording conversations (especially in 2-party consent states)
- Don't be creepy (recording strangers without context)
- Respect privacy (no bathroom/locker room recording, obviously)

Meta designed LED to be always-on when recording (can't be disabled) for transparency.

- 6-8 hours wear time
- Charge every 1-2 days

**Most users:** Charge overnight, use all day, never worry.  
**Power users:** Keep charging case in bag for mid-day boost.

**Q: Is Meta AI actually useful or just a gimmick?**

**Where it works well:**

**Where it struggles:**
- Misunderstands you ~30% of the time (noisy environments)
- Can't handle complex questions
- Sometimes "sees" wrong objects (AI hallucination)
- Requires internet (doesn't work offline)

**Bottom line:** Not a phone replacement, but useful for specific tasks. Improves monthly with software updates.
